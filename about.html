About the Band

Skipping Mondays started the way most bad ideas and all good bands do: four strangers, one borrowed rehearsal room, and a shared suspicion that the week begins one day too early.

We come from four hometowns on three continents — Hanoi, New York, Mexico City, and the German Rhineland — and it shows: warm synths argue with loud guitars, honest words settle it.

We have exactly one rule, and it is in the name.
