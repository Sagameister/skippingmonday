“Call In Sad” hits one million streams

One million of you pressed play on our saddest, sparkliest song. We wrote it on a Tuesday, recorded it in a kitchen in Neukölln, and never expected it to leave the building.

Thank you for every playlist add, every voice note, every questionable karaoke rendition. The next single is already whispering at us from the rehearsal room.

Köln show sold out — second night under discussion

Gebäude 9 is officially full. Köln, you did that in eleven days, which our booking agent says is “not normal” — we choose to take that as a compliment.

02.06.2026

Studio diary: week one of album two

Day one: set up the synths. Day two: argued about a tambourine for five hours. Day three: the tambourine won. Album two is going to be honest, loud, and 4% percussion politics.

Producer Mel Okafor has banned the word “vibe” from the control room. Progress has improved measurably since.

Berlin, Hamburg, Köln, München, Wien. Five rooms we love, zero early mornings, all shows starting fashionably late. Tickets are live now.
